Frequently Asked Questions

Meet the dedicated professionals working behind the scenes

Job Portal is a comprehensive platform that connects job seekers with employers. You can create a profile, upload your resume, search for jobs using various filters, and apply directly to positions that match your skills and experience. Employers can post job vacancies, manage applications, and find the right talent for their teams.

Creating an account is simple. Click the "Register" button in the top navigation, choose your role (Job Seeker or Employer), fill in your details, and you're ready to go. You can also sign up using your existing social media accounts.

Yes, Job Portal is completely free for job seekers. You can create a profile, search for jobs, and apply to positions without any charges. We believe that finding your dream job should be accessible to everyone.

Employers can post job vacancies by registering as an employer, navigating to their dashboard, and clicking "Create New Job." Fill in the job details including title, category, location, salary range, and required skills, then publish the listing.

Job seekers can filter by keyword, job category, location, employment type, experience level, salary range, and work arrangement. Multiple filters can be combined to narrow down the perfect job opportunity.
